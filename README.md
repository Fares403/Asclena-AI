# Asclena AI

### Transparent, Evidence-Grounded Medical AI

Asclena AI is an advanced clinical decision-support system designed to eliminate the “black box” problem in medical artificial intelligence.

Traditional medical AI systems often provide predictions without explanation. While they may achieve high accuracy, they fail to answer the most important clinical question:

> **Why?**

Asclena is built around a fundamentally different philosophy.
Instead of relying on a single monolithic model, it separates medical reasoning into three independent yet collaborative components:

* **Perception** – Quantitative medical image analysis
* **Interpretation** – Clinical semantic understanding
* **Verification** – Evidence-based scientific validation

This structure transforms AI from a prediction engine into a transparent, evidence-grounded reasoning system.

---

## The Core Idea: From Prediction to Verified Reasoning
Most AI systems generate results directly from learned patterns.
Asclena treats model outputs as **hypotheses — not final answers.**

Every clinical claim must pass through an evidence-verification layer before it becomes part of the final report.

This ensures:

* No unsupported medical statements
* No hallucinated explanations
* No overconfident conclusions
* Clear citation-backed reasoning

The result is not just a prediction — but a structured, auditable clinical report.

---

## How Asclena Thinks

Asclena operates as a multi-stage reasoning pipeline:

### 1. Medical Image Analysis (Perception)

The system analyzes medical scans such as X-rays or CT images to extract quantitative diagnostic signals and localization maps.

### 2. Clinical Interpretation (Interpretation)

The system generates structured clinical observations in natural language, describing findings and potential implications.

### 3. Scientific Verification (Agentic Evidence Layer)

All findings are treated as verifiable claims.
Each claim is checked against a curated medical knowledge base.

Only evidence-backed statements are allowed into the final report.

If evidence is insufficient, the claim is either softened with uncertainty or removed entirely.

---

## Why This Matters

Medical AI must be:

* Accurate
* Interpretable
* Evidence-based
* Safe

Asclena introduces an **Evidence-Gating Rule**:

> No clinical claim enters the final report without scientific support.

This shifts the system from being a prediction tool to becoming a **transparent clinical reasoning assistant** aligned with real-world medical governance standards.
